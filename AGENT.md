# Agent Guide

- Keep `src/YYYYMMDD/`, `docs/YYYYMMDD/`, and `docs/index.html` synchronized.
- Do not commit API keys or private customer data.
- Public repo may include generated summaries and images from official Google Cloud blog posts, but avoid full copyrighted article extracts.
- Verify at least title, SEO/OG meta, `Noto Sans KR`, three `.blog-image` references, and image assets before commit.
